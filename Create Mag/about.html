About us

The creative industries are one of the UK’s fastest growing sectors. It contributes over £80 billion to the UK economy. There has never been a more exciting time to join the UK’s thriving creative industries. These industries need young people with transferable skills. “The creative industries are those industries that are based on individual creativity, skill and talent” source: Department for Culture, Media & Sport Here at Create Mag we want you to realise your dreams of a career in the creative industries and we will be featuring different practitioners, studios and developments in the creative industries Creative Skillset helps the Creative Industries to develop skills and talent by providing finance. They will help you to find out more about the different creative industries and the opportunities open to you. If you’ve got the talent, Creative Skillset has a range of options that will give you a road map into the creative industries.
