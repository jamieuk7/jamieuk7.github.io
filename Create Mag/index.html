What is CreateMag

Create Mag is a new monthly online magazine aimed at students who want a career in the creative industries. The aim of the magazine is to support young people who are studying subjects that are focused on creative computing by:Sharing practical techniques, Showcasing studentsâ€™ practical work, Writing about developments in the creative industries and Allowing students to share their views and opinions on aspects of the creative process.
